The Last Supper (Il Cenacolo)

The Last Supper is a monumental mural that captures the dramatic moment when Jesus reveals to his disciples that one of them will betray him. This painting is renowned for its masterful composition, which organizes the apostles into groups of three, creating a sense of harmony and balance. The expressive faces of the apostles, combined with the innovative use of perspective to draw the viewer's eye towards Jesus, have made it one of the most studied and iconic religious artworks in history. The mural is also noted for its detailed depiction of emotional reactions and interactions among the figures.
